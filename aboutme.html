Why choose Duc Le?
With Duc you will get high quality work with the most affordable rate. As a member of Pacificwide Business Group, Bay Area Association of Realtors, and California Association of Realtors, Duc is experienced in representing both buyers and sellers in the real estate market. Duc’s expertise entails up-to-date market activity, including active, pending, sold listings, and MLS Hot Sheet or Activity Report. Duc also offers complete service in Vietnamese. Duc’s professional work includes:

  • Completion of real estate documents, agreements, and records with the proper state agencies
  • Development of marketing plans for listings and designing fliers, newsletters, and other promotional collateral
  • Providing buyers with options, inform them of correct market prices, and help them make informed decisions
  • Coordination of appointments, open houses, showings, and meetings with clients and other real estate agents
Duc finds his true passion in fulfilling the needs of his clients. Duc was also in your shoes when he bought and sold his first home. He understands your needs and worries. He will go above and beyond to ensure that you are comfortable and confident with your decision, be it to buy or sell. He is honest, professional, and straight to the point with his advising. He answers phone calls and replies to emails quickly. For both buyers and sellers, his goal is to always get you the most return value for your investment.

Buyers’ satisfaction is Duc’s first and foremost concern. A Bay Area native himself, Duc understands your lifestyle and is attentive to what it demands, whether this means a shorter commute, safer neighborhood, better school district, or cleaner air— Duc will assure you that your needs will be his number one priority. Duc also offers his buyers free reports about the neighborhood of interest, early access to unpublished listings, and daily market updates.

As for sellers, Duc will provide you with in-depth market studies and a free detailed home evaluation. His goal is to always keep you in the loop so that you are confident in your knowledgeability. He will help you attain the best price possible for your home by achieving the most marketable presentation of your home, assist you in advertising tactics, and provide free advertisement through online publishing, social media postings, and virtual tour video recordings.

Duc is also fluent in Vietnamese and takes pride in helping his Vietnamese community in the Real Estate market. He will assist you in signing important documents as well as learning about the market. With him as both your realtor and interpreter, you will find absolute confidence and comfortability in having him by your side.
